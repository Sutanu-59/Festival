--:::MUHARRAM:::--

Muḥarram (Arabic: ٱلْمُحَرَّم) (fully known as Muharram ul Haram) is the first month of the Islamic calendar.[1] It is one of the four sacred months of the year when warfare is forbidden.[2] It is held to be the second holiest month after Ramadan.
The Islamic calendar is a lunar calendar, and months begin when the first crescent of a new moon is sighted. Since the Islamic lunar calendar year is 11 to 12 days shorter than the solar year, Muharram migrates throughout the solar years.
