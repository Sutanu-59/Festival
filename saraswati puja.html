--:::SARASWATI PUJA:::--

Saraswati Puja is one of the most important festivals of Hindus in the eastern region of India. It is performed of the occasion on Basant Panchami or VasantPanchami. Goddess Saraswati is the Goddess of learning, wisdom, music, arts, and science. The festival is held in Magh, the month of the Hindu calendar, which falls in January or February. It is celebrated by all, especially the youngsters who seek blessings for academic excellence and success from the Goddess.
The Puja was celebrated with full devotion at the IMI Bhubaneswar campus. As IMI is an educational institution, it is one of the most important festival. This Puja was attended by the students, staff and faculty of the institution. Arrangements such as the Saraswati statue, puja samagri, the purohit, prasads, etc. were made.
